2009 Urban Ministries Missions Conference

Seminar Workshop Information

"Randy Nabors has been the PCA's champion for holistic, gospel-centered, community-based ministry for nearly 30 years. He was preaching it and doing it long before all the rest of us got into gear."
- Rev. Sandy Willson
Mercy Ministry
by Randy Nabors

Randy presents a theological and practical explanation of the church's ministry of mercy.

Leadership development in a cross-cultural context
by Elliott Greene

Hear about Dr. Greene's ministry to identify and encourage the future leaders of a reformed movement among African-Americans and Hispanics.

Understanding the diversity of our neighbors
by Josh Geiger

Learn about the challenges of reaching a city of multiple cultures, from Josh's experience of ministering to our Hispanic neighbors.

Work and ministry
by Bill Peel

Find new ways to leverage your skills for the peace of the city. Bill describes how God can use your work background to serve the Kingdom in Dallas.

Mo Leverett founded Desire Street Ministries in New Orleans in 1990 and served as it executive director fand president until Septemeber 2006 when he founded Rebirth International, an organization with a focus on the revitalization of urban communities worldwide. Mo's work is still recognized as one the best new models for community revitalization in this nation.
Raising up leaders from the inner city
by Mo Leverett

Mo shares his reflections on more than 20 years of ministry in inner-city New Orleans and addresses the challenge of developing leaders.

Abstinence: the road to freedom from sexual bondage
by Mary Flo Ridley

Are you finding it difficult to address abstinence in ministry? Mary Flo has prepared a seminar geared specifically toward reaching children in an urban culture. This seminar will share practical ways to explain God's plan for purity.

From Rahab to Ruth: walking with women leaving incarceration
by June Groom

A former women's prison warden and a Christian leader, June shares how to uniquely minister to women dealing with incarceration.

Cultural warriors: enduring transformation
by Steve Vanderhill

Find how a Christ-centered seminary is integral to developing urban leaders who will faithfully engage our city with a strong biblical worldview.
